How it works

Two homes a year.
Two paychecks a year.

Each year, one pair closes. The first home sells the normal way — a bank pays you cash at closing. On the second home, you hold the loan at 6% interest. Monthly checks come to you for seven years, then the buyer refinances and sends the remaining balance in one final payment.

Your protections

You are protected
at every step.

  • 🏠
    You hold the mortgage until paid in full.
    On every seller-financed home, if a payment is missed the property reverts to you. You keep what was paid and take back the house.
  • 📋
    Every price is locked in writing before we close.
    A Master Purchase Agreement covers all 8 properties, with agreed prices and a closing schedule, legally binding from day one.
  • 💰
    Cash at every closing — no waiting.
    Once a year, a pair closes and you receive a cash payment at the table. Actual money in your account that day.
  • 🔒
    The final payoff is required by contract.
    Seven years after each closing, the buyer must refinance and pay the remaining balance. If not, they're in default and you reclaim the property.

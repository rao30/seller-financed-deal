The 8-Duplex Portfolio — Deal Analyzer

Cashflow, returns, and tax-shield modeling for the 8-duplex portfolio. Buy-and-hold with REPS, stabilized rents to $1,850/unit, and the price-shift seller-finance structure.

Price shift baked in: The seller's pair total is honored exactly ($3,248k across all four pairs). $50k of equity per pair is shifted from the bank side to the SF side, the SF stated rate is set to 6.0% (IRS AFR), and the recorded SF deed is auto-derived so payments + 7-yr balloon equal the seller's ask — yielding an effective 0% rate to the buyer while creating a real REPS-side interest deduction. Bank side carries a 2% seller credit; SF side carries a $25k repair escrow at close (LLC-invoice method, deposited to HYSA). Edit any Sale Price below to override.

Credits, Liquidity & Tax Shield

Price-Shift Mechanics

The SF repair escrows ($25k × 4) flow to the LLC at close and sit in a 5% HYSA until each property turns. The bank seller credits absorb closing/prepaid costs on the four bank closings. The interest you'd "pay" on each SF note never leaves your pocket (it's already netted into the deed price) but it generates a deductible loss the wife's REPS status can convert into W-2 tax savings.
